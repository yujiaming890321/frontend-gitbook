## take ... with a pinch of salt

pinch /pɪntʃ/ v.拧，捏，掐；
salt /sɔːlt/ n.盐
take sth with a pinch of salt 对某物保留意见
we should take these numbers with a pinch of salt.  对于这些数字，我们不可尽信
his opinion should be taken with a pinch of salt. 他的观点，我们要有所保留的听取
